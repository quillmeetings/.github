## Quill

**Your AI Chief of Staff** — from conversation to execution.

Quill captures your meetings, transcribes them with multi-speaker precision, and
turns them into notes, answers, and follow-ups. No bot joins your calls: Quill
runs on your own device and captures audio at the system level, so it works
offline and **your data stays yours**.

- 🌐 [quillmeetings.com](https://quillmeetings.com)
- 📥 [Download Quill](https://quillmeetings.com/download)

### Open source

Most of our work lives in private repositories, but a few things are public:

- **[quill-claude-extension](https://github.com/quillmeetings/quill-claude-extension)** — Quill's Claude extension
